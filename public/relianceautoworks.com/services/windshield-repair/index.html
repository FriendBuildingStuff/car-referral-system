Windshield Repair in Brooklyn, NY

Clear Your View of the Road

Vehicles may be evolving rapidly, but certain components stand the test of time. One of the most important is the windshield. A car’s windshield is made from a specialized form of glass—it’s not the same kind as a glass cup, for example. Often, the glass is a concoction of fine silica sand, soda ash, cullet, limestone, and dolomite, and in some formulations, potassium oxide and aluminum oxide may be used. Windshields allow you to see the road ahead and behind and drive safely without obstruction. However, windshields also act as a protectant from outside forces. Weather, sure, but also in collisions and other incidents. If your windshield is compromised in any way, though, your safety is also compromised. This is why proper windshield repair is necessary when needed. Our Windshield Repair Services

If you think about it, your windshield endures a lot on the road. While windshield glass is designed to withstand many of the everyday road hazards, such as bad weather or flying debris, damage can incur at the right angle and velocity. A windshield is still glass, and it’s not indestructible. Even severe changes in temperature and pressure, and installation errors can wreak havoc and warrant windshield repair. The most common types of windshield damage are cracks and chips, and more often than not, they can be fixed. If the crack is longer than three inches in length or the chip is greater than one inch in diameter and penetrates through, the glass will need to be replaced. Other types of damage include bulls-eyes (impact from round objects) and stars (small chips with multiple cracks). Rest assured that whatever the damage may entail, we have the tools, the expertise, and the dedication to perform sound windshield repair service. If total glass replacement is needed, we can also take care of that for you.
